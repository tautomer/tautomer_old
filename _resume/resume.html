Education

University of Rochester
Ph.D. in Computational ChemistryAdvisor: Pengfei (Frank) Huo 2015 - Now

My work has focused on understanding chemical reaction mechanisms through the reaction rate calculations from molecular dynamics simulations.

  • Combine ab-initio classical and ring-polymer molecular dynamics with efficient enhanced sampling techniques to investigate the reaction mechanism of proton transfer reactions.
  • Provide new theoretical insights on cavity modification of the ground state reactivity in the vibrational strong coupling (VSC) regime in polariton chemistry.
Wuhan University
B.S. in ChemistryAdvisor: Quan Yuan 2010 - 2014

My work was related to nanoparticles synthesis and their applications on biological systems.
